About Us

We create beautiful, handmade willow coffins for families looking for a gentler, more sustainable choice. Each coffin is woven by hand by Olivia - an experienced basket maker and tutor - using willow grown on our small farm in Pembrokeshire.

Our coffins are designed with natural burial grounds in mind: simple, elegant, and free from synthetic materials.

Whether you're planning ahead or arranging a funeral for someone dear, we provide a calm, personal service and craftsmanship you can trust.

Sustainable Materials

All of our coffins are handmade using beautiful, natural and environmentally-friendly materials.

Willow

Unlike conventional coffins, which depend on processed timber, metal fittings and energy-heavy manufacturing, our willow grows back each year, needs no replanting, and can be worked entirely by hand. It's a material we can harvest, handle and weave ourselves, by hand - we don't use machinery in any of our processes.

Our willow begins life in the soil of our small Pembrokeshire farm where we grow a large number of traditional willow varieties. Local soils influence the colour, vigour and texture of willow and we have carefully selected our varieties for their beauty and suitability for basketry and coffin weaving.

Willow is exceptionally fast-growing and we harvest each winter. We harvest our willow by hand, allowing us to give the plants the care they require and to select the finest rods for weaving. These rods are then graded, dried and stored for use throughout the year.

Where necessary, we also source premium willow from trusted growers in Somerset. These growers provide the buff and white willow rods as these require specialist treatment, and to meet demand while we expand our willow beds.

Finish

OSB or all-willow, woven base.

Willow coffins are fully biodegradable making them ideally suited for natural burial sites.

Willow regrows after a single year and can be harvested indefinitely.

Each coffin is made by hand and finished with natural rope handles, wooden toggles, and an unbleached cotton lining.
